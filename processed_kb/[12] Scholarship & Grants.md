## I. Overview

Xavier University - Ateneo de Cagayan provides various scholarships and financial grants to support students who  demonstrate  academic  excellence,  leadership  potential,  or  financial  need.  These programs are administered by the Admissions and Aid Office (AAO) and the University Scholarship Committee, which oversee the selection and management of scholarship recipients.

Scholarships  may cover tuition,  school  fees,  allowances, or other academic expenses, depending on the type of scholarship granted.

## II. Types of Scholarships

## President's Scholarship

The  President's Scholarship  is awarded  to  top-performing  applicants  based  on  the  university scholarship examination.

## Benefits:

- Full coverage of tuition and school fees
- Additional allowance for books, uniforms, or ICT-related expenses

## Merit Scholarship

The Merit Scholarship is awarded to applicants who perform exceptionally well in the scholarship examination but rank slightly below the President's Scholars.

## Benefits:

- Full coverage of tuition and school fees

## Academic Scholarship

The  Academic  Scholarship  is  granted  to  students  who  achieve  high  scores  in  the  scholarship examination and meet the university's academic standards.

## Benefits:

- Full tuition coverage
- Coverage of matriculation fees

## St. Ignatius of Loyola Scholarship

The St.  Ignatius  of  Loyola  Scholarship  is  awarded  to  the  Top  1  or  Top  2 students of a senior high school graduating class with a sufficiently large batch.

## Benefits:

- Full tuition coverage
- Full matriculation coverage

## St. Francis Xavier Scholarship (Grant-in-Aid)

The  St.  Francis  Xavier  Scholarship  is  a  financial  assistance  grant  intended  for  students  who demonstrate both academic ability and financial need.

Eligibility Requirements:

- Passing score in the scholarship examination
- Family income within the university's financial eligibility threshold

## Benefits:

- 25,000 pesos coverage for tuition expenses.

## IV. Administration of Scholarships

Scholarship programs at Xavier University - Ateneo de Cagayan are managed by:

- Office of Scholarships and Grands (formerly Admission and Aid Office)
- University Scholarship Committee

These office are responsible for:

- Processing scholarship applications
- Screening applicants
- Monitoring scholarship compliance
- Implementing scholarship policies.
