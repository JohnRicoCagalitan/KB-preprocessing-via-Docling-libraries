## XAVIER UNIVERSITY SCHOLARSHIPS

## A. Academic Scholarships

The  Xavier  University  Academic  Scholarship  is  awarded  to incoming freshmen, who are bonafide citizen of the Philippines, based on the Entrance Exam (RTCU). There are three categories under this:

- 1) XU President's Scholarship Grant (TOP 25) Coverage: Full tuition, matriculation, other fees, Php 10K allowance for books and uniforms

2) XU Merit Scholarship Grant (TOP 1%)

Coverage: Full tuition, matriculation and other fees

3) XU Academic Scholarship Grant (TOP 9%)

Coverage: Full tuition and matriculation

Steps in Applying Academic Scholarship Step 1. Pre-Registration online admission / Scholarship application through XU website

Step 2. Take the required XU Qualifying Entrance Examination/RTCU

(Schedule for Entrance Exams:  Every Saturday of November)

## B. St. Ignatius Loyola Scholarship Grant

## (Take and pass the required XU Entrance Exam)

Coverage:

Full Tuition and Full Matriculation (for Val)

Half Tuition and Half Matriculation (for Sal)

## Steps in Applying St. Ignatius Scholarship Grant

Step 1. Pre-Registration online admission / Scholarship application thru XU website

Step 2.  a) Take the required XU Qualifying Entrance Examination/RTCU

(Schedule for Entrance Exams:  Every Saturday of November)

b) Qualified applicant for Val/Sal scholarship needs to submit the following documents:

- i. Letter of Application addressed to the Director of Admissions and Aid, Xavier University, Cagayan de Oro City.
- ii. Certification from the High School Principal stating that the applicant is the High School Valedictorian or Salutatorian with a total number of 50 High School Graduates.
- iii. Copy of School Graduation Program

Step 3. Proceed to enrolment process

## C. St Francis Xavier Grant-in-Aid

The  St Francis Xavier Grant-in-Aid is the University's response to provide quality education to poor but deserving students. It is a financial aid for qualified incoming Freshmen whose academic performance in high school is above average, with a General Scholastic Aptitude (GSA) Score of 86-94% in the XU Scholarship Exam (RTCU) and whose total annual family income does not exceed to Ps 500,000.00 for a family of  5  or  per  capita  income  of  Ps  100,000  as  evidenced  by  a certified true copy of the latest annual Income Tax Return or where not applicable, a statement from BIR of tax exemption with attached affidavit of sources of income.

Coverage: Ps 15,000.00 per semester

## Steps in Applying St Francis Xavier Scholarship

Step 1. Pre-Registration online admission / Scholarship application thru XU website

Step 2. Take the required XU Qualifying Entrance Examination/RTCU

(Schedule for Entrance Exams:  Every Saturday of November)

- a. Result of the XU Entrance Exam will be released first week of January through XU Website using the password given to each examinee.

b. Qualified applicant for XU scholarship needs to submit online and attached clear scanned copy of the following documents: Deadline of application is on January 30, 2018

Disclaimer: The following requirements are pre-requisite for the approval of your scholarship application.

i. Two filled  out  Recommendation forms one from the HS Principal and Class Adviser/Guidance Counselor (Downloadable online)

ii. Income  Tax  Return  of  both  parents  or  if  not applicable  BIR  statement  of  Tax  Exempt  with  attached Affidavit of Sources of Income iii. Latest 1x1 Picture

- iv. Print Scholarship Offer Letter and bring hard copy of the scanned documents on Feb 10, 2018 for the interview with the Scholarship Board Committee
- v. Receive notice via Email of Scholarship Award

vi. Submit downloaded  Confirmation slip on or before 1 st week of March 2018 for contract signing

Step 3. Proceed to enrolment process
