## Online Admissions &amp; Enrollment Process for Returnees 2403 Intersession

Higher Education Online Admissions and Enrollment Procedure for Intersession (formerly Summer) Upperclassmen XU Returnee* Applicant

RETURNEES refer to Xavier Ateneo upperclassmen who intend to enroll for a particular semester but were not enrolled during the previous semester/s. Returnee applicants must not have transferred to another school or university during the un-enrolled semester/s.

## Step 1

Access the Link to register for the evaluation process for RETURNEE applicants.

## Step 2

Wait for the notification via email for the approved ADMISSIONS CLEARANCE FOR RETURNEES . If you do not see the email, check your 'junk mail' folder or spam folder.

## Step 3

Pay the required down payment of Php 1,500.00 Head over here to view the list of accredited Xavier Ateneo payment centers.

-   IMPORTANT: Secure the ADMISSIONS CLEARANCE first before making a down payment. The instructions for accomplishing the bank forms are indicated on the Admissions Clearance.

## Step 4

Head over here for the Online Enrollment Registration .

Prepare clear photos or scanned copies of the following documents for uploading during the online registration:

- Returnee's Admissions Clearance
- Validated Down Payment Deposit slip
- Signed Data Privacy Consent Form (downloadable from the enrollment registration form)

After submitting the online form, kindly wait for the email about the status of your enrollment registration. If successful, you will receive the login credentials to your Student Lifecycle Management and Information System (SLMIS) account. Class schedules and other related information can be viewed in the SLMIS.
