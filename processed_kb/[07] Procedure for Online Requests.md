## Procedure for Online Requests

## OPTION A

1. The student downloads the Request Form. Please click here .
2. The student fills in and signs the form.
3. The student gets access to the link: https://forms.gle/1khAJLW7wTVgas4M8 , fills in the necessary data and uploads the ff:
- a) Scanned copy of the completed online request form.
- b) Scanned copy of front and back sides of student's XU ID or any government-issued ID.
- c) Scanned copy of a 2' x 2' photo with white back ground for students of the Juris Doctor program or if the purpose of the request is for board/bar exam
4. The Registrar's Office conducts initial clearance of the account of the student at the Finance Office and notifies those who still have unsettled financial obligations
5. The student waits for the assessment of payment by the registrar on the document and courier fee. The documentary fee depends on the highest obtained degree while the courier fee depends on the location of the delivery address.
6. The student makes a payment deposit for the assessment given by the registrar with the ff details:
7. The student gets access to the link: https://bit.ly/36PsrJ6 , fills in the necessary data and uploads a copy of proof of payment with the student's name written on it and the online assessment form.
8. The Finance Office confirms the payment and issues an official receipt.
9. The Registrar's Office notifies the student on the status of his request. Turn-around time for the TOR is 5-14 working days from date the payment was cleared and posted by the Finance Office while that of the diploma is 1 month.
10. The Registrar's Office secures clearances from the ff offices: Library, Dean's Office, Office of Student Affairs, Book Center, Finance Office and Guidance and Counseling Office (additional for transfer credentials) . Requests will not be processed if student has incomplete credentials (F138, F137-A, Official TOR from previous school, Transfer Credential, etc.) The registrar will notify those who still have unsettled financial and/or property obligations with the forementioned offices.
11. The Registrar's Office sends the requested documents via LBC or DHL to the student's address indicated in the request form.
