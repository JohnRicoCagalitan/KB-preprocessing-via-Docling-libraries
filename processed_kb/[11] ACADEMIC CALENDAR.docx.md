## ACADEMIC CALENDAR

## Xavier University - Ateneo de Cagayan School Year 2025-2026

## First Semester

## Enrollment

Enrollment for freshmen will be from April 15 to August 2, 2025 . Enrollment for all year levels will be from July 21 to August 2, 2025 .

## Orientation The orientation of freshmen and transferees will be from July 30 to August 2, 2025 . Enrollment Adjustments

Delayed enrollment will be from August 4 to August 9, 2025 . Adding, dropping, or changing of classes will be from August 11 to August 16, 2025 .

## Class Sessions

Class sessions for the First Semester will be from August 4, 2025 to December 18, 2025 .

## Examination Schedule

The Preliminary Examination will be from September 8 to September 13, 2025 . The University Retreat will be from September 17 to September 19, 2025 . The Midterm Examination will be from October 6 to October 11, 2025 . The Semi-Final Examination will be from November 10 to November 15, 2025 . The Final Examination will be from December 9 to December 15, 2025 . The Delayed Final Examination will be from December 16 to December 18, 2025 . The Due Date of Grades will be on December 22, 2025 .

## The Christmas Vacation

will be from December 19, 2025 to January 11, 2026 .
